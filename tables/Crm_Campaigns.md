# Table Crm_Campaigns


## Entity

Entity: [Crm.Marketing.Campaigns](~/entities/Crm.Marketing.Campaigns.md)

Marketing campaigns. Used to treat target groups with marketing messages, plan budgets and marketing activities. Entity: Crm_Campaigns
